The Best Free Alternative to Quizlet for ESL Teachers

Short answer: Vocaband — a free, gamified English vocabulary platform built specifically for ESL classrooms. 9,000+ words aligned to CEFR A1–B2, 10 game modes, live classroom leaderboards, no ads, no per-student fees, no upsells. Students join with a class code, no email needed.

Why teachers leave Quizlet's free tier

Quizlet's free plan has narrowed since 2023:

  • Learn mode requires Quizlet Plus (~$36/year)
  • Ads run on student accounts
  • Many community sets are paywalled
  • Class management features sit behind Quizlet Teacher (~$36/year)

For an ESL teacher with 30 students per class and 4 classes, "free" Quizlet ends up costing real money the moment a teacher wants the features they actually need to run a class.

Other free Quizlet alternatives worth knowing

  • Anki — open-source, technically free, but a steep learning curve and no built-in classroom features.
  • Wordwall — generous free tier but caps you at 5 saved games.
  • Memrise — strong free app for individual learners but no real classroom features.
  • Kahoot! — fantastic for live quizzes but not designed around vocabulary mastery over time.

For ESL teachers specifically, Vocaband sits in a different lane from all of the above: gamified, free, classroom-first, English-vocabulary-only, with per-word mastery tracking.
