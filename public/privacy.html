Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2024 | Version: 2.0

Legal Basis: Privacy Protection Law, 5741-1981 (Israel), Amendment 13 (תיקון 13 לחוק הגנת הפרטיות)

Summary: Vocaband is designed for Israeli schools. Student accounts are anonymous—no email or personal identification required. Teachers sign in with Google. We don't sell data, show ads, or track users for marketing.

1. Data Controller (בעל המאגר)

Under the Israeli Privacy Protection Law (Amendment 13), the data controller for Vocaband is:

  • Entity: Vocaband Educational Technologies
  • Address: Israel
  • Privacy Contact: contact@vocaband.com
  • Database Registration: As required under Section 18 of the Privacy Protection Law

For privacy inquiries, data access requests, or complaints, contact us at the email above. We will respond within 30 days as required by law.

3. Data We Collect

3.1 For Students (Anonymous Accounts)

Student accounts are designed to be anonymous. We collect:

  • Display name — chosen by the student (does not need to be a real name)
  • Avatar — an emoji selected by the student
  • Class code — the 6-digit code used to join a class
  • Game scores and progress — scores, game modes played, word-level mistake data
  • Badges and achievements — in-app accomplishments
  • Streaks and XP — engagement metrics

We do NOT collect: Student email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses, photos, government IDs, or any personally identifiable information (PII).

3.2 For Teachers (Google OAuth)

  • Email address — used to verify teacher identity against a pre-approved allowlist
  • Display name — from Google profile
  • Classes created — class codes and settings
  • Assignments created — vocabulary lists and due dates

3.3 Technical Data (Automatic)

  • Session tokens — for maintaining login (stored in browser local storage)
  • IP addresses — for security and rate limiting (not stored long-term)
  • Browser type — for compatibility (not used for tracking)

6. Data Retention Periods

Under Amendment 13, we retain data only as long as necessary:

Data Type Retention Period Deletion Trigger
Student progress data 365 days Class deletion or account deletion
Orphaned student accounts 90 days after last login Automatic cleanup
Teacher accounts Active use + 2 years Account deletion request
Audit logs 2 years Automatic deletion
Consent records Duration of account + 3 years Legal requirement

When a class is deleted, all associated assignments and progress records are automatically removed (cascade delete). Backup data may be retained for up to 30 additional days.

8. Children's Privacy

Vocaband is designed for students in Israeli schools, who may be minors under applicable law.

8.1 School Authorization

The educational institution (school) authorizes student use of Vocaband as part of the educational curriculum. By providing a class code to students, the teacher (acting on behalf of the school) authorizes student access.

8.2 Minimal Data Collection

We minimize data collection from students:

  • No email address required for students
  • No real name required (display name only)
  • No personal identification documents
  • No location tracking
  • No behavioral advertising

8.3 Parental Rights

Parents or legal guardians may exercise data subject rights on behalf of their children by contacting the school or emailing contact@vocaband.com with verification of guardianship.

All transfers are made with appropriate safeguards as required by Israeli law and international standards.

10. Security Measures

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.3)
  • Encryption at Rest: Database encrypted by Supabase
  • Access Control: Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • Authentication: Teachers authenticated via secure Google OAuth; students use class codes
  • Teacher Verification: Only pre-approved email addresses can access teacher accounts
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against automated attacks
  • Audit Logging: All data access and modifications are logged

11. Cookies and Local Storage

Cookie Preferences Remember cookie consent choice 1 year

We do NOT use: Tracking cookies, advertising cookies, third-party analytics cookies, or social media pixels.
