Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Vocaband ("we", "us", "the App") is a vocabulary learning application designed for use in Israeli schools. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially students who are minors.

1. Data We Collect

For Students (anonymous accounts):

  • Display name — chosen by the student at login (does not need to be a real name)
  • Avatar — an emoji selected by the student
  • Class code — the 6-digit code used to join a class
  • Game scores and progress — scores, game modes played, and word-level mistake data
  • Badges earned — in-app achievements

We do not collect student email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses, photos, or any government-issued identifiers. Student accounts are fully anonymous.

For Teachers (Google OAuth):

  • Email address — used to verify teacher identity against a pre-approved allowlist
  • Display name — from Google profile
  • Classes and assignments created

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide the vocabulary learning experience (games, scores, progress tracking)
  • To show teachers their students' scores and progress in a gradebook
  • To power real-time leaderboards during live challenges
  • To authenticate teachers via Google OAuth

We do not use data for advertising, profiling, marketing, or any purpose beyond operating the App.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication provider. Data is stored on Supabase-managed servers. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Google OAuth — used only for teacher sign-in. We receive the teacher's email and display name. No student data is shared with Google.
  • Render — application hosting provider.

We do not use any analytics, tracking, advertising, or data-broker services. No third-party scripts collect user data.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption in transit)
  • Database access is protected by Row-Level Security (RLS) policies
  • Teacher accounts require pre-approval via an allowlist
  • Student sessions are anonymous — no personally identifiable information is required
  • Server-side rate limiting protects against abuse

5. Data Retention

Student progress data is retained for the duration of the school year or until the teacher deletes the class. When a class is deleted, all associated assignments and progress records are automatically removed (cascade delete).

6. Your Rights

Students, parents, and teachers have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of their data
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of their data

To exercise these rights, contact your school administrator or email us at the address below.

7. Children's Privacy

Vocaband is designed for use in schools under teacher supervision. Student accounts are anonymous and do not require personal information. The school (as the educational institution) authorises student use of the App. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without school authorization.

8. Cookies and Local Storage

The App uses browser local storage to:

  • Maintain authentication sessions (so users don't need to log in repeatedly)
  • Temporarily store unsaved scores for retry if the network is unavailable

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party cookie services.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
